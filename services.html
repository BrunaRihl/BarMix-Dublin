Services

Mixology Workshops


Interested in learning the art of mixology? Our intimate mixology workshops are perfect for smaller groups of up to 10 people. Led by our expert bartenders, these interactive sessions dive into the world of cocktail making. You'll learn the techniques, ingredients, and secrets behind crafting delicious and visually stunning drinks. Get hands-on experience as you shake, stir, and garnish your own creations, all while enjoying a fun and educational experience with friends or colleagues.

Event Bar Services


Planning a private event or a special celebration? Let us take care of the bar services! Our professional bartenders will bring our fully stocked bar to your venue, ensuring that your guests have access to a delightful array of cocktails, premium spirits, and personalized drink menus. We'll work closely with you to curate a bar experience that matches the ambiance and theme of your event, leaving you free to enjoy the festivities.

Mobile Mixology


Our mobile bar truck brings the party to you! Whether it's a vibrant music festival, a lively street fair, or a corporate event, our mobile mixology team will set up a fully equipped bar and serve a wide range of handcrafted cocktails to delight your guests. With our lively bartenders and stylish mobile bar setup, we bring the bar experience to any open-air gathering.
