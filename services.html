Services

Mixology Workshops


Interested in learning the art of mixology? Our intimate mixology workshops are perfect for smaller groups of up to 10 people. Led by expert bartenders, our interactive sessions explore the art of cocktail making. Learn techniques, ingredients, and secrets for crafting delightful drinks. Get hands-on experience and enjoy a fun, educational time with friends or colleagues.

Event Bar Services


Planning a private event or a special celebration? Let us take care of the bar services! Our expert bartenders will bring a fully stocked bar to your venue, offering delightful cocktails, premium spirits, and personalized drink menus. We'll collaborate with you to create a bar experience that complements your event's ambiance and theme, so you can fully enjoy the festivities.

Mobile Mixology


Our mobile bar truck brings the party to you! Whether it's a vibrant music festival, a lively street fair, or a corporate event, our team will set up a fully equipped bar and serve a wide range of handcrafted cocktails to delight your guests. With our lively bartenders, stylish mobile bar setup and handcrafted cocktails served with flair we bring the bar experience to any open-air gathering.
